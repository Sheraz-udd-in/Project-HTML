WELCOME TO RAILWAYS

India has some of the most spectacular and unforgettable rail journeys in the world. Here you experience a simple way to find out everything you need to know in one easy place.
A renovated face of train travel of modern India.
A platform where you primarily book your train tickets online apart from planning your holidays, order meals, and book train tourism packages. Railways developed an objective to enable a single window access to information and services being provided by the various Railways entities.

Railways are the principal mode of transportation for freight and passengers in India. Railways also make it possible to conduct many activities like business, sightseeing, and pilgrimage along with transportation of goods over longer distances.
